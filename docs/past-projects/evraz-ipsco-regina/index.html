REGINA BEACH YACHT CLUB (RBYC)

2 Level Yacht Club to enhance the rich tradition of the RBYC

Features of this design include:

  • 2600 sf Upper Level containing a theme ‘boat’ bar, Yacht Club Dining and Club Lounge; extensive 2300 sf Upper Level deck and a full commercial kitchen to cater to the Club clientele
  • Unrivalled view of Long Lake and Qu’Appelle valley created by extensive traditional craftsmen style windows; sets up a panoramic view that unites upper space with natural beauty of the 90 year old lake front property
  • Lower Level contains Meeting Rooms, Mid-Level Exterior deck, full change facilities, Yacht Club Retail Shop, Storage Amenities with seasonal Staff Suite 2011 flood was devastating; with loss comes new energy and excitement that will breathe new life into the classic Regina Beach recreational experience

Location

Regina Beach, Saskatchewan

Project Budget

$1.2 million

Completion Date
